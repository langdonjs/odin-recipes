French Toast

The best breads for French toast are brioche, sourdough, French bread, or challah. These varieties are dense and sturdy enough to handle total saturation in the wet, milky, egg custard without falling apart. However, in a pinch, any thick-sliced white bread will do.

  • White Bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla and Cinnamon
  • Salt and Butter
    Step 1Gather all ingredients. Step 2Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  1. Step 3Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat.
  2. Step 4Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  3. Step 5Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  4. Step 6Serve hot.
